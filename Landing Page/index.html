SWITZERLAND

Lake Lucerne is a lake in central Switzerland and the fourth largest in the country.Lake Lucerne is also noteworthy for the variety of scenery

THAILAND

Temples play an important role in Thai everyday life.Many tourists especially enjoy visiting the stunning riverside temple Wat Arun in Bangkok and the pale white temple Wat Rong Khun in the northern province of Chiang Rai.

MALDIVES

Maldives is world-famous for its wide spectrum of marine life, and one of the best ways to explore it is by snorkelling or scuba diving. The activities allow you to swim freely in the Indian Ocean to explore the colourful arrays of corals, reef sharks, sea turtles, stingrays, and others.
